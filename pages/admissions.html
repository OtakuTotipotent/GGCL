Intermediate Classes Admission Rules & Regulations

  • The admission for intermediate class will be offered on the dates advertised in the National Press and notice board of the college.
  • The admission is offered on merit and keeping in view the number of seats ; allocated for the respective disciplines.
  • A candidate must apply online for F.A/F.Sc admission from above link and also deposit the challan form in the bank.
  • A candidate will also purchase the Prospectus available in college campus and fill the admission form.
  • The admission form must be furnished with:
    1. Challan form
    2. Four passport size attested photos of the candidate
    3. Two attested copies of the provisional/character certificate
    4. Two attested copies of the CNIC of the father of the candidate
    5. Three copies of matric result card
    6. The admission is offered as per policy of the Government of Punjab.
    7. The original Degree/ certificate/ result card must be submitted shown to admission committee at the time of interview.
    8. NOC (No Objection Certificate) is required from the students who have been registered in other than BISE D.G.Khan and BZU Multan.
  • The fresh candidates are eligible to seek admission in the classes of first year. The students failed in some subjects are not allowed to be admitted as per rules of Board of Intermediate and Secondary School Certificate.

BS-4 Years Classes Admission Rules & Regulations

Disciplines Offered:

  1. BS-Botany
  2. BS-Chemistry
  3. BS-Mathematics
  4. BS-Physics
  5. BS-English
  6. BS-Urdu
  7. BS-Islamiat
  8. BS-IT

Eligibility:

A candidates who has passed intermediate examination from any Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education of Pakistan or an equivalent examination recognized by the university with one elective subject corresponding to offered major discipline chosen by the candidate, is eligible for admission to BS (4-Year) in the offered major disciplines (1st semester) provided that the candidate has secured at least 45% marks in the subject of his/her chosen major discipline as well as in the aggregate of Intermediate of equivalent examination.

Computation of Merit

The merit will be calculated as aggregate marks in F.A./F.Sc. or equivalent plus marks in the concerned subject plus 20 marks for Hifz-e-Quran.
